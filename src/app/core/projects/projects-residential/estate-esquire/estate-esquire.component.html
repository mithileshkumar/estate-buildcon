Overview

Esquire historically was a title of respect accorded to men of higher social rank. Estate Esquire was built on the ideology of creating a place which would command instant respect. A place you would be proud to call your home.

This project of 100 flats holds 4 BHK, 3 BHK and 2 BHK options in four blocks. Be its serene ambience, 65% open garden & recreational space, top quality materials used in and out; all elements come together to create its splendour, a place worthy of respect.

Estate Esquire was a huge success and is considered to be one of the best residential complexes in Ranchi.
