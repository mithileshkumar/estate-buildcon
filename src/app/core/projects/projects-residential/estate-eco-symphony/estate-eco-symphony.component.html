Overview

Luxurious Sustainable Living.

Estate Eco Symphony is a premium residential project of 139 Flats. In Estate Eco Symphony, we have combined luxury with environmental sustainability.

It is the first “Gold Rated Green Building” project of Jharkhand, and has all the modern amenities you could ask for.

We have secured water for your future with comprehensive waste water recycling and reuse system, 100% rain water harvesting system and many more.
