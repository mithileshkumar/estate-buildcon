Overview

Estate Esquire II is a smaller replica of the splendor of Estate Esquire I. The apartment has 32 3-BHK flats, each of approx 1580 Sq ft.

Amenities

  • Community hall - 3100 sq. ft.


  • Intercom


  • 24hrs. Generator


  • Security


  • Overhead water tank fitted with sensor for automatic filling

Specifications

  • Floor
    All rooms, balconies, passage and bathroom finished with vitrified/ceramic/anti-skid tiles and three inches skirting.


  • Structure
    Building with earthquake resistant R.C.C. structure. Exclusive use of TATA TISCON FE 500 steel bars only.


  • Kitchen
    Granite working platform and ceramic tiles in dado upto 24" height over the platform. Stainless steel sink with opening for exhaust fan.


  • Door
    Flush doors with salwood frame.


  • Windows
    3 way glazed aluminium windows with net & MS safety grills.


  • Water Supply
    24 hours water supply from own deep tube well boring, to be stored in over-head water tanks.


  • Telephone and T.V.
    One telephone line point, and one T.V. cable point provided in living room and master bedroom.


  • Lift
    8 passenger automatic lift with stainless steel finish (2 in each block).


  • Exterior Finish
    Acrylic weather proof paint for exterior finish.


  • Fire fighting System
    With security features.


  • Floor Height
    10'6" (finishing 10').


  • A/C
    Provision for AC in all rooms.


  • Stairs Landing & Steps
    Green marble flooring.
