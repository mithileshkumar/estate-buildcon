Overview

Mangal tower a G+6 floors commercial project was completed in 2008. The building holds shop spaces in the ground and first floor, and offices in the remaining floors.With construction area of about 75,000 sq ft and an impressive design, its demeanour stands imposing on the face of the road.

The complex has ample parking space with double basement parking for shop and office owners, and large frontal space for visitors.

Apart from well-designed office and shop spaces, significant building frontage and imposing looks are aspects which made the project an immediate success.
